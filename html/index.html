Milk & Honey Teahouse

Policies

Technology

  • In keeping with our elegant interpersonal atmosphere, we do NOT provide wifi and are NOT a study/ work space.

  • We endeavor to provide a stress-free environment for all of our guests, so that they can fully enjoy the afternoon tea experience. Therefore, we request that you silence your cell phone upon and entry, and conduct ALL phone calls outside or on the screen porch.

Children


  • Due to our formal atmosphere, fragile china, and VERY hot liquids. Children MUST be 5 years old to attend an afternoon tea. In the same vein, no strollers or high chairs will be permitted into the establishment.

  • Although we do understand that accidents happen, parents will be held liable for any breakages and the additional charge will be added on to the total of the bill.

Outside Food


  • We will NOT permit any outside food or beverages.

Reservations/ Cancelation


  • Because afternoon tea is time and labor intensive we require a 'holding' (50%) deposit at the time of reservation. Please notify us at least 90 minutes before reservation of ANY changes, this will help us to serve you better. Reservations cancelled after the start of the 90 minute window will be charged the holding fee

Full/ Partial booking


  • Afternoon tea is limited to large parties of 12, unless the party doesnt mind being seated at multiple nearby tables.

Smoking and Vaping


  • Smoking and vaping are prohibited on the property

Allergies


  • We can provide gluten free scones and sandwiches. However, that is unfortunately all that provide
